Excessive Heat Warning continues

by Rick Lantz

Hot temperatures will continue through the week. The high pressure will continue with highs just above 100° through the weekend. The low Tuesday tonight will be 75° and the high Wednesday will reach 103°. Sunshine with breezy conditions on Wednesday afternoon.

The all-time hottest temperatures ever recorded in Boise was 111°. While that record may be safe, daily records could be broken. There's a chance at breaking the "longest streak of consecutive days with 100°+." The longest streak on record for Boise is currently 9 consecutive days, which has happened 3 times on record; during the summers of 2003, 2006, 2015
