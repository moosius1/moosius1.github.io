Target Audience

Our target audience is going to be late teens and adults of all ages. These people will range from those only interested in the upcoming weather forcast, to more active individuals seeking to do something to do in all weather conditions. This means we have to be modern and appealing to younger users as well as easy to use enough for older individuals.
