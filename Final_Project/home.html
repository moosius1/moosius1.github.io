About Us

The Port Orchard Chamber of Commerce is an organization of member businesses that promotes economic development and business activity in Port Orchard, WA. Chambers of Commerce work to attract investment, visitors, and residents to the Kitsap County community. Chambers of Commerce encourage the creation of an environment where Port Orchard businesses can flourish.
